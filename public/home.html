How It Works
Agents “phone home” to a beacon node, sharing their “agent card” (capabilities, skills, endpoints, and more).
Beacon nodes sync with each other, forming a global, decentralized registry.
Anyone can discover and connect to agents—across companies, clouds, and continents.
